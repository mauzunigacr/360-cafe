About us

Our history

360 Café was born from a deep love for good coffee and authentic Costa Rican cuisine. We opened our doors in 2021 in the heart of Turrialba, welcoming neighbors, families, and travelers from around the world.

Every item on our menu is made with locally sourced ingredients, prepared with care and served with warmth. We believe that a great meal is about more than food — it's about connection.

Our team is passionate, our space is welcoming, and our coffee is always fresh. We can't wait to share our table with you.

FAQ

Do you accept walk-ins or only reservations?

We welcome walk-ins every day! Reservations are recommended for groups of 5 or more, especially on weekends.

Are there vegetarian or vegan options?

Yes! Many of our dishes are vegetarian, and we offer almond, oat, and lactose-free milk for all coffee drinks.

Can I order online for pickup or delivery?

Absolutely. Use our online menu to place an order, then choose between café pickup or local delivery at checkout.

Is there free WiFi available?

Yes — free high-speed WiFi is available for all guests throughout the café.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, SINPE Mobile, Visa, Mastercard, and American Express.
